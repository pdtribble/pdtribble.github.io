PhishWise

A cybersecurity awareness training platform for running realistic phishing simulations and measuring employee security behavior.

View on GitHub

overview

PhishWise is a full-stack cybersecurity awareness platform built to help organizations identify vulnerabilities in their human attack surface. It lets administrators create and launch realistic phishing campaigns, track who clicked, who reported, and who submitted credentials — then automatically delivers targeted training to those who fail.

The platform is designed with a security-first mindset: all campaign data is stored locally, email templates are customizable, and results are broken down by department or role for meaningful reporting.

key features

  • Campaign builder with customizable phishing email templates and landing pages
  • Real-time dashboard tracking click rates, credential submissions, and report rates
  • Role-based access control for admins and campaign managers
  • Automated remediation training triggered on failure events
  • Department-level analytics with exportable CSV reports
  • Target list management with import/export via CSV
  • Audit log for all admin actions and campaign events

technical details

Built with Node.js + Express on the backend, using SQLite for local persistent storage. The frontend is server-rendered with vanilla JS for interactivity — no client-side framework, keeping the deployment footprint small.

Email delivery is handled via SMTP with support for configurable relay servers. Tracking pixels and unique per-target redirect URLs enable per-user click attribution without relying on third-party services.
